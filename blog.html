What I do:

This is where I store My review and commentary content. I cover everything from Music, to Video Games, Movies, and TV. I usually focus on underground material. This means that a lot of this is overlooked, low-publicity, obscure, or old to the point where it has been forgotten and This blog allows me to give more of my perspective on these topics. I also welcome critique and commentary from my users on here. Feel Free to tell me what you think. Genres covered here are often left out of mainstream publications and include specialties such as extreme metal, underground hip-hop and rock, as well as the undercurrents of the electronic scene. I also cover independent video games, lesser known films, and cult TV shows.
