Nicholai's Bunker

Underground gaming and music

This site of mine is about my interests in underground music, games, and movies with as much attention and diligence I could give to these forms of media. It's stuff that often goes unlooked at and many people miss out on cool things that just slip under the radar. I bring you these with the hope of expanding your palate a bit and showing off some buried treasure that I've unearthed (or some unholy abomination that's too bad to let go ignored). This is also to offer my thoughts on the various pieces of media that get thrown around a lot inconversations. This is my take on modern culture. Sure, I will include occasional nods to the mainstream, but for the most part, this blog dwells in the underground and in the forgotten past. Welcome to my bunker, don't worry about the dust.
